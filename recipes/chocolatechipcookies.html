Chocolate Chip Cookies

Heavenly Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

These delicious cookies were named, "Heavenly Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies," because they have a special ingredient - marshmallows!

Chewy on the inside and crispy on the outside, this marvelously perfect cookies will have them coming back for more. They are a definite favorite at my house, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 c. butter, softened
  • 3/4 c. granulated sugar
  • 3/4 c. packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 c. mini marshmallows
  • 1 c. coarsely chopped nuts, if desired

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â° F.
  2. In a small bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugars on medium speed for about 1 minute or until combined and creamy - remember to periodically scrape down the sides of the bowl to make sure everything is getting combined.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Add flour mixture, a little at a time, just until blended. Again, remember to scrape down the sides of the bowl as you go.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips, then nuts (if added), and finally the marshmallows. Note: If dough is too soft, cover the bowl and place dough in the refrigerator for about an hour. This will make the dough easier to scoop out when preparing to bake.
  7. Place parchment paper on a large baking sheet. Drop rounded spoons full of dough (about 2 tablespoons each) onto the baking sheet, about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake 8 - 10 minutes or until light brown. The center will be soft. Let cool for 2 minutes then remove them from the cookie sheet and place on a cooling rack. Cool completely, about 30 minutes. Store covered in an airtight container.
